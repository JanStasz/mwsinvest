About MWS Invest

An independent partner for serious, long-term capital.

We invest where we have deep roots and real conviction. Operating from Warsaw and Switzerland, we combine local insight with international standards to identify opportunities others overlook.

Our approach is patient and hands-on. We back resilient businesses and well-located real estate, protect the downside, and let disciplined decisions compound over time.
